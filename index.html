OUR STORY

For Reuben and Kylla coffee has always played a part in their relationship. They first met in a cafe, and Reuben would chat over the counter with Kylla who was a barista at their church. Several coffee dates later, they would even elope in a cafe. Since the beginning of their relationship, they always dreamed of opening their own cafe in the heart of downtown Homewood, where they had their first kiss.
Honey's Cafe is prodcuct of their love story, passion for all things coffee, and a love for their neighborhood.
